Mafia Game

Mafia — Original Method

Drawing on practices that improve wellbeing and years of experience in event hosting, I created a unique format designed around self-discovery, emotional intelligence, and genuine connection between guests.

10 players. Each guest is invited to the round table individually — accompanied by a presentation with pre-prepared facts and music. Name cards help guests find their footing and connect on a personal level.

The host gradually introduces the group to the rules and techniques that help players trust their own feelings. Up to 3 sessions can take place in one evening: if there are 30 guests, each plays once; if there are 10, each participates three times. After all sessions, nominations are awarded.

Duration

3–3.5 hours

Price

from €600

Quizzes

Multimedia Programme

Using a TV screen and guests' phones to vote, a supporter-stand atmosphere takes shape — lively, but contained. The programme includes live quizzes with questions about the guests themselves, a guess-the-tune round, and pseudo-intellectual challenges built around whatever the crowd is into — from Eurovision to the World Cup.

The programme moves at pace, lifting spirits and putting guests at ease. The multimedia block sets a playful, light-hearted tone.

Wellness

Self-Discovery Through Movement

At 5, I began exploring what I felt. For the first fifteen years I intuitively learned to understand my emotions and inner experiences. In the second half of my life I explored various therapeutic approaches hands-on: personal and group psychotherapy, psychodrama camps, sessions with specialists in the helping professions, art therapy, meditation, and dance therapy.

The simplest answers and real results — changes in health and the quality of everyday life — began to emerge once working with the body was added to exploring the psyche. Working with energy — meditation — brought further clarity. Drawing on thirty years of accumulated experience, I began helping others, choosing formats where not only knowledge but also lived experience matters.

This practice can be light and easy — waking the body in the morning, setting a positive tone for the day — or, when needed, more intensive: learning to recognise and express feelings, better understand the body's needs, and discover your own authentic qualities.

Suitable for
  • Those who want to slow the mind and release tension from the body
  • Those who want to strengthen their connection with the body and build self-confidence
  • Groups of friends or work teams who want to deepen their connection with one another

Duration

1.5 hours

Price

€25 per person
